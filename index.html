Wellness Information


  1. Stay in touch with people making sure to make friends and socialise, make sure you allow yourself to be supported and support others. It will increase your self worth and can be as easy as a Google Meet chat, a phone call, anything that means not isolating yourself.
  2. Try going for a walk and getting some fresh air or some other physical activity as it can really improve your mood.
  3. If you're bored, try learning something new — maybe even a skill — to keep your mind active and positive.
  4. Be mindful, focus on the now and take in everything around you, and you can improve your thoughts and feelings.
  5. Relax, try to find time and space to just be and feel good. Try not to worry yourself all the time.

Information.


Approximately 22.6% of adults have experienced some sort of mental illness and around 10% of children have too. Women experience this at a higher rate than men and younger adults are more prone to mental illness. It effects all types of people from different walks of life and can strike at any time. about 551,000 people have a more severe mental illness like bipolar disorder. It can be very negatively impactful and can be long lasting. Please remember you are not alone and seek help if you feel you need it.
